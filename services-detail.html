The World's Largest Dog Breeds

Dogs, one of our precious and lovable friends with whom we share every moment of our lives, can appear in giant sizes as well as in tiny sizes. Let's take a look at our giant sized dog friends!

Our dog friends, which we come across at every point of our lives, at home, on the street, at any time, and whom we strange when we cannot see, can appear in tiny sizes as well as giant sizes.

These harmless creatures, which can frighten people with their large dimensions, have a giant heart in direct proportion to their size. They are wonderful creatures that you should love, play and feed, not fear.

Although there is a belief that it has a coarse structure due to its large size, the English Mastiff, which is a very gentle, docile and affectionate dog breed, is one of our cute dog friends who can protect his owner and his area with great courage with his protective instinct despite his very rare bark. For these reasons, the English Mastiff is a great guard dog.

Why Should Dog Hotel Be Used?

Dogs are social creatures who are extremely fond of their owners. That's why they don't like being alone. At this point, dog hotel service comes to the aid of our dog friends and owners!.

Dogs are extremely social creatures. They want to constantly interact with other creatures. Dogs, who are such special and social creatures, do not like being alone. Often times, dogs that are not very mild to anyone other than their owners can cause quite annoying situations in the absence of their owners.

Some dog species are introverted and not very social creatures, unlike other species. They only interact with their owners and with people or other animals they have seen for a long time next to the owner. For this reason, being alone at home or spending time with a stranger can be described as a very painful process.

They deserve everything perfect. As Pet Clinic, we are focused on overcoming all obstacles for you. Love is the best medicine.
